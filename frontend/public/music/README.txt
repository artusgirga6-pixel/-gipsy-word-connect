Drop your Romani / Gypsy MP3 tracks here. Recommended naming:

  /music/track-1.mp3
  /music/track-2.mp3
  /music/track-3.mp3
  /music/track-4.mp3
  /music/track-5.mp3

When tracks are present the in-game MusicPlayer will play them in rotation
(switching every 5 levels). When the folder is empty (only this README), the
game falls back to a procedurally-generated Gypsy-style melody.

Recommended sources for royalty-free Romani / Balkan folk music:
  - Pixabay Music     (https://pixabay.com/music/search/gypsy/)
  - Free Music Archive (https://freemusicarchive.org/genre/Balkan)
  - YouTube Audio Library (CC tagged)

License compliance is your responsibility before publishing the app.
